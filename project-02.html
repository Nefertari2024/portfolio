在这两个项目里，我做了什么 My Role Across Both Projects

🗂️
内容策略制定 Content Strategy
从零梳理展厅与视频的整体内容框架，确定叙事逻辑、信息层级与呈现顺序，把"要说什么"转化为"怎么说、说多少、按什么顺序说"。 Built content frameworks from scratch — defined narrative logic, information hierarchy, and presentation order. Translated "what to say" into "how, how much, and in what order to say it".
🤝
多方协作管理 Multi-party Coordination
作为甲方核心对接人，统筹协调外部设计公司、施工团队、广告公司拍摄团队，平衡多方意见，确保执行方向与内容策略一致。 As client-side core liaison, coordinated external design firms, construction teams, and ad agency video crews — balancing perspectives to keep execution aligned with content strategy.
✅
全流程质量把控 End-to-End Quality Control
作为最终成品审核人，把控展厅落地效果与视频成片质量，及时发现并协调解决执行偏差，确保最终交付与预期高度一致。 As the final reviewer, controlled the quality of the physical exhibition and final video cuts — promptly spotted and resolved execution gaps to ensure delivery matched expectations.

这两个项目让我真正理解了一件事——把复杂的事情讲清楚、让一群人按同一个方向把它做出来，本身就是一种核心产品能力。这种能力可以直接迁移到数字化场景：将SAP IBP、S/4HANA等复杂系统的使用方法转化为清晰可读的操作手册、培训材料与思维导图，让业务方真正理解和使用，是数字化产品经理的关键工作之一。 These two projects made me truly understand one thing — making complex things clear and aligning a group of people to deliver in one direction is itself a core product capability. This skill transfers directly to digital contexts: turning the usage of complex systems like SAP IBP and S/4HANA into readable user manuals, training materials, and visual guides that business users genuinely understand and adopt — one of the key responsibilities of a digital product manager.
