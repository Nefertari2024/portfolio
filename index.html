我相信，数字化产品经理的核心价值不在于技术本身的炫目，而在于对真实业务场景的敏锐洞察、跨部门的有效沟通，以及让复杂工具真正被业务方接受并用起来的落地能力。 I believe the core value of a digital product manager lies not in the glamour of technology, but in keen insight into real business scenarios, effective cross-functional communication, and the ability to make complex tools truly accepted and used by business partners.

我对数字化产品的理解 My Take on Digital Products

🔍
从真实痛点出发 Start from Real Pain Points
好的数字化产品不是技术的展示台，而是对真实业务痛点的精准回应。我习惯从"这件事为什么麻烦"开始思考，而不是从"这个技术能做什么"出发。 A good digital product is not a showcase for technology, but a precise response to real business pain. I prefer to start from "why is this troublesome" rather than "what can this technology do".
🧩
复杂系统中的局部创新 Local Innovation Within Complex Systems
在已经成熟的数字化体系中，产品经理的价值不在于颠覆，而在于找到那些"技术上已经具备、但实际上没用起来"的能力缝隙，并用本地化的方式让它们落地。 Within a mature digital ecosystem, the value of a product manager lies not in disruption, but in spotting capability gaps where the technology is already there, but the adoption is not — and bringing them to life through localized execution.
⚡
快速迭代的产品思维 Iterative Product Thinking
从V1.0到V3.0的自主迭代经历让我深刻理解：产品不是一次性交付物，而是在真实使用中持续进化的过程。发现问题、定义改进、快速验证，是我的工作节奏。 The V1.0 to V3.0 self-iteration journey deepened my conviction: a product is not a one-shot delivery, but a process of continuous evolution through real use. Identify, define improvements, rapidly validate — this is my rhythm.
